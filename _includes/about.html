About

Dewberry Bees is a.Dewberry Bees is a small, independent business based in Nailsea, North Somerset. Run by a local beekeeper (that’s me!), I care for my own hives and produce local wildflower honey and natural beeswax products. Everything is made in small batches, with care and respect for the bees and the environment.

Our bees do more than make honey — they help protect the environment. With our “Adopt a Bee” and hive sponsorship packages, you can support pollinators and play a part in preserving the local ecosystem. Whether you're after a sweet treat or want to give back to nature, you're in the right place.
